Last Updated: June 24, 2026

1. ACCEPTANCE OF TERMS

By engaging with the Bangkal Milk Bank (the "Service"), you agree to the terms and conditions outlined in this Donor End-User License Agreement ("EULA"). If you do not agree, you are prohibited from proceeding with the donation process.

2. DONATION GUIDELINES & SAFETY

All human milk donations are subject to strict screening protocols. The Donor acknowledges that donated milk will undergo Holder Pasteurization. The Service reserves the right to reject any donation that does not meet safety standards without prior notice.

3. MANDATORY SEROLOGICAL SCREENING

Donors must undergo mandatory blood testing for HIV, Hepatitis B, Hepatitis C, and Syphilis. Failure to provide accurate results or undergo screening will result in immediate termination of the donation agreement. All costs are covered by the Service.

4. DATA PRIVACY & USAGE (RA 10173)

Donor data is collected and processed in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012. Records are strictly for medical tracking. The Service will not share your personal identification with third parties without express written consent.

5. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

The Service is not liable for any adverse reactions resulting from the donation process itself, provided that standard medical protocols were followed. Donors certify they are in good health and under no prohibited medication.
